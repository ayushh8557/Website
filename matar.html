Matar Paneer

Matar paneer is a popular vegetarian dish from North India. It features paneer (cottage cheese) and green peas cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy. The paneer is typically cubed and added to a spiced sauce with the vibrant green peas, resulting in a rich and aromatic dish. It's often seasoned with a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, creating a delicious balance of flavors. Matar paneer is commonly served with naan, roti, or rice, making it a beloved dish in Indian cuisine appreciated for its creamy texture and savory taste.

step 3
