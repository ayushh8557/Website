Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish that is enjoyed by people of all ages. It is a fermented crepe made from a batter of rice and lentils.There are many different variations of dosas, including masala dosa, rava dosa, and many more 12. Masala dosa is a crispy crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture, while rava dosa is made with semolina instead of rice 2. Dosas are known for their crisp texture and tangy flavor, thanks to the fermentation process. They are usually served with a side of coconut chutney and sambar

prep time

2hrs

cook time

15min

serving

1 servings

instructions

step 1

Make the batter: Soak rice and urad dal in water for several hours, then grind them into a fine paste. Add salt and let the batter ferment overnight.

step 2

Prepare the dosa: Heat a griddle or tawa over medium heat. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the center of the griddle and spread it out in a circular motion. Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook until the bottom is golden brown. Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is crispy.

step 3

Serve: Serve hot with a side of coconut chutney and sambar.
